Seafood Pancake Recipe

Image credit: Photo by AMANDA LIM from Pexels

Description

A simple seafood pancake with shrimp, squid, and green onions. It is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup shrimp
  • 1/2 cup squid or other seafood
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Steps

  1. Put the flour, egg, water, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix until smooth.
  3. Add the shrimp, squid, and green onions.
  4. Heat the oil in a pan.
  5. Pour the mixture into the pan and spread it evenly.
  6. Cook until the bottom is golden brown.
  7. Flip the pancake and cook the other side.
  8. Cut into pieces and serve warm.
