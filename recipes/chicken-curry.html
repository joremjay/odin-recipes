Chicken Curry Recipe

Image credit: Photo by Nonik Yench from Pexels

Description

A simple chicken curry made with chicken, potatoes, and carrots. It is rich, flavorful, and good to eat with rice.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 2 potatoes, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pot.
  2. Cook the onion until soft.
  3. Add the chicken and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the curry powder and stir well.
  5. Add the potatoes and carrots.
  6. Pour in the water and let it boil.
  7. Lower the heat and cook until the chicken and vegetables are tender.
  8. Add salt to taste, then serve.
