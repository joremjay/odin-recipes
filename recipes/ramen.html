Ramen Recipe

Image credit: Photo by Su La Pyae from Pexels

Description

A simple ramen dish with noodles, broth, pork, and egg. It is warm, tasty, and easy to make for a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack ramen noodles
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • Egg
  • 2 slices cooked pork
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili oil or sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Boil the water or broth in a pot.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook for about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the soy sauce and oil, then stir.
  4. Place the noodles and soup in a bowl.
  5. Add the cooked pork, egg, and green onions on top.
  6. Serve while hot.
